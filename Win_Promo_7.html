Jared: How did the program work for you?


Jacquelyn: The program guides you into feeling you're not alone in your career and where you are. It's empowering because it leads you day-by-day to discover your value within yourself and how to excel within your career and take action. [Jared and Leslie] lead you through specific pillars and homework assignments.

It was life-changing, and I enjoyed the program. I will take everything — all of the value and mentorship — with me throughout my career.

What was different about this program for you?


What was different about it was that everyone was working toward servant leadership. So there's more meaning to your career than just coming in and punching in and punching out. 

There is true change you can bring to the world. This program is a mentorship within that. 

So it means more to me than just trying to improve at what I do. It's trying to bring change — positive change — to the world and people. That is true success.


What were things like before the program?


Before I signed up for the program, I had difficulty showing my value within the organization and having people see what I could bring to the table. So I was sort of chipping away at that stone.


With the program, I have identified specific action-taking examples to situate myself among executive leadership. I’m now noticed as a person of value with skills, ideas, and solutions. 


I’m seen for my problem-solving solutions, for what they want: their objectives and goals. I learned how to listen better and how to understand what people need.



What is different for you now that you have gone through the program?


It's easier to do the work that I do and make a bigger impact because people see my own personal brand and my team's brand differently. So we are becoming more of a group of researchers, a group of problem solvers, a group of storytellers, and we're more effective in the manner in which we work and are efficient.
